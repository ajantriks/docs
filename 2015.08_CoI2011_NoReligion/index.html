Where did respondents not mention their religion?

The chart below is based on Census of India 2011 data. In the chart, the size of rectangles indicate the percentage of people in the state concerned not mentioning their religion (out of total state population), and the colour of the rectangles indicate the absolute number of respondents in that state who did not mention their religion (red is high, light gray is low).

The national percentage of respondents not mentioning their religion is 0.237%, with the highest percentage among states being 0.48% (Arunachal Pradesh), and the lowest being 0.081% (Delhi).
