Where did respondents not mention their religion? Urban versus Rural

The chart below is based on Census of India 2011 data. In the chart, the X (horizontal) axis presents percentage of urban population of the state concerned that did not mention their religion, and the Y (vertical) axis presents percentage of rural population of the same state that did not mention their religion.

The highest percentage among urban areas is 0.772% (Andhra Pradesh), and the lowest is 0.081% (Delhi). The highest percentage among rural areas is (Arunachal Pradesh), and the lowest is 0.041% (Chandigarh).
